FREE VIDEO LESSONS

Here you can view 6 free video lessons that I have written and produced. These lessons will help you get familiar with the basics needed to get started on the violin.

With time there will be more video lessons here, but I also recommend that you visit the website www.violinmasterclass.com which has many free video lessons on all the different technical aspects of playing the violin. The most efficient way to learn the violin is to take private lessons every week, and practice practice practice. But video lessons can provide a different perspective, and should be looked at as a supplement to your private lessons. Have fun!
