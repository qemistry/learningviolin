0 = Open string (no finger) 1 = Index finger 2 = Middle finger 3 = Ring finger 4 = Pinky finger (where you put your fingers) A whole step / half step simply refers to the distance between two notes. A half step is half the distance of a whole step (how easy is that?) If you are playing a full size violin, a whole step is approximately an inch. For a half step, the fingers are right next to each other touching. (This is all approximate because it depends on the size of your fingers.)
